This interactive dashboard has been created so that the results from our BellyButton experiment can be viewed by the researchers as well as the participants. The subjects are left anonymous by using their Subject ID Number. With each participant, this dashboard will display their Ethnicity, Gender, Age, Location, BBTYPE, and their washing frequency. The charts on this dashboard will show the top 10 bacteria found in their Bellybutton, their washing frequency, as well show the various bacter cultures found in their bellybuttons.
